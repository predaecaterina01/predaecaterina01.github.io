01 Who We Are

A surface is not
decoration.
It is specification.

Deco Preda is a specialist architectural surface contractor operating across the United Kingdom and Ireland. We deliver engineered surface systems — from precision mineral applications in private residences to high-performance resin flooring across logistics infrastructure at industrial scale.

Our work spans two distinct verticals. In residential, commercial and hospitality environments, we apply microcement, decorative resin, stone carpet and hybrid surface compositions that combine technical performance with uncompromising aesthetic intent. In infrastructure and logistics, our high-performance resin systems are specified for extreme compressive loads, chemical resistance and continuous heavy-traffic use — from 50m² private commissions to facilities exceeding 10,000m².

Every project — regardless of scale — is governed by identical standards: certified European material systems, EN-protocol application procedures, full technical documentation and post-installation support.

Execution Quality EN-standard application protocols. Multi-coat systems. Full substrate preparation documentation at every stage.
Material Integrity Certified European mineral and resin systems — selected for long-term performance, durability and regulatory compliance.
Industrial Scale Capacity, logistics and technical management from 50m² residential commissions to 10,000m²+ infrastructure projects.
Design Versatility Custom resin patterns, hybrid compositions, bespoke mineral tones and graphic wayfinding systems — no two projects identical.

04 Craft
EN Standard
Protocol

The precision
of specification.
Applied by hand.

Every surface system follows a strict multi-stage application protocol: substrate assessment and preparation, primer application, base coat, surface coat, and final protective layer. Each stage is documented, inspected and signed off before the next begins.

Our specialist application teams operate to EN-standard protocols with full technical documentation, system warranties and post-installation support. The same team that assesses your specification applies your surface — no hand-offs, no subcontracted application.
