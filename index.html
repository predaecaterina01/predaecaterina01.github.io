05 About Us

Precision at every
scale. Quality
without compromise.

Deco Preda was established with a single conviction: that architectural surfaces — whether in a private residence or a multi-thousand-square-metre industrial facility — deserve the same rigour of material selection, technical execution and aesthetic intent. We do not separate decorative work from functional work. We apply the same standard to both.

Our specialist teams are trained in microcement, decorative mineral plaster, cast resin flooring systems and stone aggregate — materials chosen for their resilience, versatility and capacity for custom design. For industrial and commercial environments, our high-performance epoxy resin systems are engineered for extreme compressive loads, chemical resistance and continuous heavy-traffic use, while offering complete creative freedom in pattern, colour and finish — including bespoke geometric and graphic designs unique to each client.

Industrial logistics hubs, distribution warehouses, multi-level public car parks and large-format commercial floors represent a core pillar of our activity — environments where surface performance directly affects operational efficiency and where scale demands a contractor capable of delivering consistent quality across thousands of square metres without interruption or compromise.

Execution Quality Every surface is applied by trained specialist teams operating to strict technical protocols. The client brief drives every decision, from substrate preparation to final protection.
Material Integrity We work exclusively with certified European mineral and resin systems — selected for long-term performance, verified durability and compliance with commercial and industrial standards.
Design Versatility Seamless surfaces across floors, walls, stairs and furniture. Custom epoxy patterns, unique graphic compositions and bespoke mineral tones — no two projects identical.
Industrial Scale From 50m² residential commissions to 5,000m² warehouse and parking infrastructure. Capacity, logistics and technical management at every scale.
