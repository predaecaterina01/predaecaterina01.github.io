Where the
systems end,
the work begins.

The four surface systems we offer — Microcement, Stone Carpet, Performance Resin and Design Resin — each have defined specifications, tested protocols and predictable outcomes. Signature Series exists beyond those boundaries.

These are compositions created at the intersection of multiple systems. Microcement as a base, sealed with a transparent resin topcoat that introduces depth without gloss. Performance resin grounds and mineral aggregate floats within it. Acrylic composites that combine the elasticity of polymer with the warmth of mineral pigment. No two commissions share a formula.

Signature Series is specified in direct collaboration with the client and their architect — from initial material research through prototype sampling to final application. The surface delivered is not selected from a range. It is developed for a single space.

From brief
to surface.
The process.

01
Material Research
We review the project brief, spatial requirements and aesthetic intent. System combinations are identified and material samples are sourced from our European suppliers.
02
Prototype Sampling
A minimum of three physical samples are prepared — each exploring a different system combination, texture or finish. Samples are reviewed on-site or delivered for approval.
03
Specification & Testing
The approved composition is tested for adhesion, durability and compatibility across all layers. Application protocol is documented before any site work begins.
04
Application & Handover
Applied by our specialist team to EN-standard protocols. Full technical documentation, care instructions and a system-specific warranty are provided at handover.
